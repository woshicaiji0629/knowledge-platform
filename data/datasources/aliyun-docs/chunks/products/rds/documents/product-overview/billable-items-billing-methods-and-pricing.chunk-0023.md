### 外网连接地址流量
申请外网地址并使用外网地址连接RDS，会产生外网流量。当前通过外网地址连接RDS实例产生的外网流量（流入和流出）享受零折优惠，不收费。
功能详情，请参见[RDS MySQL](../apsaradb-rds-for-mysql/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-for-an-apsaradb-rds-for-mysql-instance.md)[开通或关闭外网地址](../apsaradb-rds-for-mysql/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-for-an-apsaradb-rds-for-mysql-instance.md)、[RDS SQL Server](../apsaradb-rds-for-sql-server/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-for-an-apsaradb-rds-for-sql-server-instance.md)[开通或关闭外网地址](../apsaradb-rds-for-sql-server/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-for-an-apsaradb-rds-for-sql-server-instance.md)、[RDS PostgreSQL](../apsaradb-rds-for-postgresql/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-on-an-apsaradb-rds-for-postgresql-instance.md)[开通或关闭外网地址](../apsaradb-rds-for-postgresql/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-on-an-apsaradb-rds-for-postgresql-instance.md)、[RDS MariaDB](../apsaradb-rds-for-mariadb/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-for-an-apsaradb-rds-for-mariadb-instance.md)[开通或关闭外网地址](../apsaradb-rds-for-mariadb/apply-for-or-release-a-public-endpoint-for-an-apsaradb-rds-for-mariadb-instance.md)。
